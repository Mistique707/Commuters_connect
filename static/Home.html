Carpool FAQs
How do I book a carpool ride?
Book easily through our web-app or on Commuter Connect. Simply enter your destination and travel date, then select the ideal carpool for your needs.
How do I cancel my carpool ride?
Plans change? No problem. Cancel your ride in the 'Your Rides' section of the app. Please cancel as soon as possible to allow others to book.
How much does a carpool ride cost?
The cost of a carpool ride varies based on factors like distance, time of day, and demand. Prices are set by the driver and can be viewed before booking.
How do I publish a carpool ride?
Offering a ride is easy! Use our app or Commuter Connect to enter your departure and arrival points, date, time, available seats, and price per seat.
What are the benefits of carpool?
Carpooling offers many advantages. It's typically more affordable than other transportation, especially for longer distances, and it's a more sustainable way to travel.
How do I start carpooling?
Getting started is simple and free! Sign up for an account, tell us a bit about yourself, and you're ready to book or offer rides through our app or website.
